Zac is a tanky AP bruiser with strong sustain and crowd control. He excels in extended fights and engages.

Skill Order

Max Order: E > W > Q

  • Q - Stretching Strikes: Second, max last
  • W - Unstable Matter: First at lv1, max second for clear speed
  • E - Elastic Slingshot: Max first for mobility and ganks
  • R - Let's Bounce!: Upgrade whenever possible

Gameplay Guide

Early Game

Focus on farming, spike on lv6 (has R) and lv9 (max E) to win combat and skirmishes.

Late Game

Look for flanks with E, disrupt enemy backline, and soak damage for your team.
