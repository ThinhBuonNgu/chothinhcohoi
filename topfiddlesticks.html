Skill Order

Max Order: W > Q > E

  • Q - Terrify: Max second for CC
  • W - Bountiful Harvest: Max first for sustain and damage
  • E - Reap: Max third for poke and extra CC
  • R - Crowstorm: Upgrade whenever possible

Gameplay Guide

Laning Phase

Bully your opponent with W sustain and E-Q-auto poke, later use crowstorm from fog to 100-0

Late Game

Patient for a great ult, either on mutiple enemies or the main carries, or use Q to peel for carries

Matchups
