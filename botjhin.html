Skill Order

Max Order: Q > W > E

  • Q - Dancing Grenade: Max first for wave clear and synergy with 4th shot
  • W - Deadly Flourish: Max second for utility and poke
  • E - Captive Audience: Max third, use for self-peel and vision
  • R - Curtain Call: Upgrade whenever possible

Gameplay Guide

Laning Phase

Use the range advantage to bully their laner, Q and W for wave clear and poke into 4th shot

Late Game

Dive head-first into the teamfight, back out and use W and R to assist the team when low
