Skill Order

Max Order: Q > W > E

  • Q - Terrify: Max first for CC
  • W - Bountiful Harvest: Max second for damage
  • E - Reap: Max third
  • R - Crowstorm: Upgrade whenever possible

Gameplay Guide

Laning Phase

Avoid getting poke out of lane, poke with E-Q, use R to roam or suprise their bot laners

Late Game

Patient for a great ult, either on mutiple enemies or the main carries, deny enemy's vision
